Our Story

Crafted for Those
Who Savour Life

Nestled in the heart of Kampala, Stonehaven was born from a singular obsession: the belief that a truly extraordinary meal is indistinguishable from art. Our chefs draw from East African terroir, weaving indigenous ingredients into plates that feel both timeless and urgent.

Our winery curates over 300 labels — from the sun-baked valleys of South Africa to the fog-draped hillsides of Burgundy — each bottle selected to companion the season's finest produce.
